Watch me Transform

Shadow has the ability to transform into pure shadow at will. When danger approaches, he melts into the darkness, becoming invisible to the naked eye. This power allows him to sneak past enemies, hide in plain sight, and travel through cracks and corners that no one else can reach. His transformation is triggered by emotion—when he feels fear or rage, the shadows respond. It’s both a gift and a curse, making him powerful but isolated.
